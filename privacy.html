Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Miaco ("the App"). We operate a peer-to-peer marketplace facilitating the exchange of cosplay items. This document explains what data we collect, how it is processed, and your rights under global privacy laws, including the GDPR (EU/UK), US State Laws (CCPA), and the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

Important Marketplace Notice: Miaco is a platform provider only. We do not process payments or handle shipping. All financial transactions and shipping arrangements are conducted externally between users. Users are solely responsible for their own transactions.

1. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information that directly, indirectly, or in connection with other information allows for the identification of a natural person.
  • Usage Data: Information collected automatically through this App (e.g., IP addresses, device operating systems, app interaction logs).
  • User / Data Subject: The individual using this Application.

2. Information We Collect and Visibility

We process information essential for delivering our services. The provision of Account Data is a mandatory contractual requirement; failure to provide it makes it impossible to use the App.

A. Publicly Visible Data

  • Username, profile picture, bio, and pronouns.
  • Linked social media handles (Instagram, TikTok).
  • Product listings (images, titles, descriptions, prices, condition).
  • User reviews and ratings.

B. Private / Shared Between Specific Users

  • Account Data: Email address (for registration and verification) and Hashed Password.
  • Communications: Direct messages (text and images) are visible only to the conversation participants.
  • System/Usage Data: Device ID, IP address, and transaction status (Active, Reserved, Sold).
No Tracking or Advertising: Miaco does NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixels, or third-party advertising networks. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (EU/UK GDPR)

  • Contract Performance (Art. 6.1.b): To create accounts, enable messaging, and display listings.
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6.1.f): To maintain platform safety, prevent fraud, and handle dispute resolution.
  • Legal Obligation (Art. 6.1.c): For tax compliance or responding to lawful requests from public authorities.
  • Consent (Art. 6.1.a): For optional profile details (like social media links).

4. Data Storage, Security & System Logs

We take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of Data. All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS, and passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.

  • Database: User data and messages are stored in a PostgreSQL database on Hetzner VPS (Germany).
  • Verification Security: Email verification codes are stored in Redis (Germany), expire after 15 minutes, and are permanently cleaned up every 60 minutes. Password reset tokens expire after 1 hour.
  • System Logs: For operation and maintenance, the App may collect files that record interactions (System logs) or use Usage Data (such as IP addresses).

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while the account is active.
  • Account Deletion (Soft-Delete): Upon request, accounts are immediately deactivated and hidden from public view.

  • Anonymization: After 12 months, personal identifiers are permanently removed. Anonymized transaction records may be kept for historical data analysis.
